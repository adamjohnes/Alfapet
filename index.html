About This Calculator

This calculator takes the dimensions of your litterbox and matches the size of your litterbox with the appropriate Alfapet sized liner. To do this, you must enter your litterbox's height, length and width in inches. Please use the pictures below for any questions you may have about properly measuring your litterbox.

Cat Pan Liner Calculator










To measure the dimensions of your litterbox, you must take the values of the length, width and height.

In cases where your litterbox may not have equal heights on all sides, measure the side with the longest height, and use that measurement as your height value.
